Exactly! You have perfectly crystallized the "Dual-Loop" value proposition of Cozy-Kart / Cozy OS.
It is a platform that rewards two distinct types of behavior, creating a self-sustaining ecosystem:
1. The Active Path: "Play to Earn"
 * Role: The Racer (The Talent).
 * Action: Joining the queue via Discord and racing in the app.
 * Incentive: The Commission Window. Every minute they race is a minute they might earn a cut of the shop sales. They are financially motivated to bring their own audience to your platform.
2. The Passive Path: "Stay to Chill"
 * Role: The Spectator (The Community).
 * Action: Keeping the Cozy OS dashboard open on a second monitor or TV.
 * Incentive: Utility & Vibes. Even if no race is happening, they stay for the Clock, Weather, Notifications, and the Starfield screensaver. This ensures that when a race does start, you already have an audience waiting in the room.

Why this works
Most apps fail because they only focus on the Active user. If no one is racing, the app is dead.
By building Cozy OS (The Passive Layer), you solve the "Empty Room Problem." You give people a reason to be there before the content starts, meaning your Racers always walk out onto a stage that is already full.
